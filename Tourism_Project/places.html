Bhedaghat - A Beautiful Natural Wonder

Bhedaghat is known for marble rocks and Dhuandhar waterfall which attracts many tourists. Bhedaghat is a famous tourist spot located about 20-25km from Jabalpur. It is well known for its stunning marble rocks and scenic beauty.

Key Attractions

  1. Marble Rocks

    • Tall white marble cliffs rise on both sides of the river.
    • The Narmada River flows between these rocks, creating a magical view.
    • Best experienced through a boat ride, especially during moonlight.

  2. Dhuandhar Falls

    • One of the most famous waterfalls in Madhya Pradesh.
    • Water falls with such force that it looks like smoke (that’s why “Dhuandhar”).
    • The sound and mist make it very powerful and beautiful.

  3. Boating Experience

    • Boat rides are a major attraction.
    • Boat guides often narrate funny and interesting stories about the rocks.

  4. Ropeway Experience

    • You can enjoy a ropeway ride over the falls for a top view.
    • Gives a thrilling and panoramic experience.

Best Time To Visit

  • October to March is Ideal
  • Evening or full moon night is the most beautiful time for boating.

Fun fact

  • Many Bollywood movies have been shot here because of its unique scenery.

How To Reach

  • Nearest City: Jabalpur
  • Easily reachable by taxi, auto, or local bus.

Ujjain - City of Temples and Spirituality

Ujjain is one of the oldest cities of India and famous for Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga temple.It situated on the banks of The holy Shpira River.

Key Attractions

  1. Mahakaleshwar Temple

    • Mahakaleshwar Temple is one of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva.
    • Famous for the unique Bhasma Aarti performed early morning.

  2. Ram Ghat

    • Ram ghat is the main ghat on the Shipra River.
    • Known for evening aarti and holy(bathing).

  3. Kal Bhairav Temple

    • Kal Bhairav Temple is famous for offering liquor to the deity.

Best Time To Visit

  • October to March(pleasant weather)
  • During festivals like Kumbh Mela and Mahashivratri

Fun fact

  • Ujjain was ruled by the great king Vikramaditya and was a major center of learning and astronomy.

How To Reach

  • Nearest major city: Indore(about 55km)
  • Well connected by road and rail

Pachmarhi-Queen of Satpura

Pachmarhi is a beautiful hill station known for its greenery, waterfalls and peaceful environment.

Key Attraction

  1. Bee Falls

    • One of the most popular waterfall in Pachmarhi
    • Fresh and clean water, perfect for tourists.

  2. Dhupgarh

    • Dhupgarh is the highest point in Madhya Pradesh.
    • Famous for sunrise and sunset view.

  3. Pandav Caves

    • Pandav Caves Pachmarhi are believed to be related to the Pandavas from Mahabharata.
    • Important historical and tourist site.

  4. Jata Shankar Cave

    • Jata Shankar Cave is a natural cave with a Shiva temple inside.

Best Time To Visit

  • October to March(pleasnt weather)
  • Monsoon(July-September) is also beautiful for greenary.

Khajuraho - Land of Temples

Khajuraho is famous historical place known for its beautiful and artistic temple. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and it is popular for its unique sculptures and architecure.

Key Attractions

  1. Khajuraho Group of Monuments

    • Khajuraho Group of Monuments
    • Built by the Chandela Dynasty
    • Famous for detailed carvings and sculptures.

  2. Kandariya Mahadev Temple

    • Kandariya Mahadeva Temple is the largest and famous temple.
    • Dedicated to Lord Shiva

  3. Lakshmana Temple

    • Lakshamana Temple Khajuraho
    • Known for its fine architecture and carvings.

  4. Light and Sound Show

    • Shows the history of Khajuraho temples in an interesting way.

    Best Time To Visit

    • October to March

    Fun Fact

    • The Temples are famous worldwide for their artistic and dedicated sculptures.

    How To Reach

    • Nearest airport: Khajuraho
    • Well connected by road and rail.

Bandhavgarh National Park

Bandhavgarh National Park is one of the most famous national park in India, located in the Umaria district of Madhya Pradesh. It is well known for having a high population of Bengal Tigers and rich biodiversity.

Key Attractions

  1. Tiger Safari

    • Bandhavgarh is famous for its jeep safari.
    • One of the best places in Madhya Pradesh, India to spot tigers.

  2. Bandhavgarh Fort

    • An ancient for located inside the park.
    • Believed to be connected to Lord Rama's time.

  3. Wildlife Variety

    • Home to animals like leopards,deer,wild boar,and many birds.
    • Dense forests and natural beauty make it attractive for tourists.

Best Time To Visit

  • October to june
  • March to May is best for tiger sightings

How To Reach

  • Nearest Railway Station: Umaria
  • Nearest Airport: Jabalpur Dumna Airport
